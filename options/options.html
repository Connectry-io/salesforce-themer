Anatomy of a Theme Card

Each theme is a complete artifact: colors, effects, typography, and favicon. Here's how to read one.

  1. 1
    Color swatch A four-stripe preview of the theme's cardinal colors — background, surface, accent, and text. Expanded in the next section.
  2. 2
    Name, category & favicon Theme name with a Light/Dark badge (auto-detected) and a small favicon — Connectry icon by default; custom themes will ship with their own.
  3. 3
    Description The mood and design language. Click the card to apply it; hover to preview the swatch.
  4. 4
    Effect pills Curated animations this theme ships with. Click any pill to jump to its explainer below.
  5. 5
    Typography Font family, size, and spacing at a glance — the "Aa" is rendered in the actual font. Full controls in the Builder's Type tab.
  6. 6
    Apply / Clone Apply uses it as-is. Clone opens the Builder. Saving custom themes is Premium.

Inside the color swatch

You met the swatch as item 1 in the Anatomy above. Those four stripes are the cardinal colors that drive the entire theme — here's exactly where each one lives in Salesforce.

Showing your active theme's colors

  • Background The page canvas — the color behind all cards and panels.
  • Surface Lifted chrome — cards, modals, the global header, the sidebar.
  • Accent Brand color — primary buttons, active tabs, links, focus rings.
  • Text Body copy and headings throughout the page.

Hover states, focus rings, modal backdrops, scrollbar tints, link colors, borders — all derived from those 23 inputs. Without the engine you'd be tuning every value by hand. With it, you pick what matters and we fill in the rest.

Effects, illustrated

Nine animations and visual flourishes you can layer on any theme. Each one is curated per theme, but you can clone any theme to customize them. Hover any card to see the effect live.

All effects respect prefers-reduced-motion and pause when the tab is hidden. Particles reduce density on battery power. Modals, dropdowns, and comboboxes are never transformed.

Questions

The short answers

Does the free tier stay free forever?

Yes. All 16 themes, auto mode, per-org theming, and shortcuts will always be free. Premium only adds new capabilities on top — nothing is taken away.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel monthly or yearly at any time and you'll keep Premium until the end of the billing period. After that you drop back to the free tier with all your OOTB themes intact.

What happens to my custom themes if I cancel?

They stay on your machine, archived. If you re-subscribe later they come right back. You just can't create or edit custom themes while on the free tier.

Why is Lifetime limited?

Early-supporter pricing. We're keeping it available while we build the product out — it rewards the folks who back us early. After the window closes it comes off the pricing page for good.

When does Premium launch?

Soon after the free tier ships. We're launching free first to get the core themes in front of users — Premium and the backend (Stripe checkout, accounts, AI features) come right after. You don't have to do anything; the Builder will simply gain a working Save button when it's ready.

Is my Salesforce data involved?

No. Themer never reads, writes, or transmits any data from your Salesforce org. Themes apply locally in your browser. Upgrading changes nothing about how the extension touches SF — it just unlocks more UI features.
